I'm a explorer by nature

I have many interests, but exploring the world is the top of them all. I love to travel to different places, city or nature. When I'm in a new place, I'm eager to wander about. Shuffling between the skyscraper in the metropolis has taught me to value time and space. Diving into the woods gives me a sniff of relaxation and tells me to balance between the outer and inner. The world itself is the greatest teacher. I'm always ready to explore, and ready to learn.
